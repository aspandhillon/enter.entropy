About Us


We are made out of our experiences, stories. These stories, we tell and re-tell ourselves, the extravagance (that's a romantic way of putting it and I think I like it).

Entropy is about laying out the patterns I have drawn, the tools I have gathered and sometimes just the beautiful things that made my day (or helped me get through one). The things I have seen and felt, been able to remember, capture and keep, courtesy of those who came and went or came and stayed.

If you find something that you were a part of, thank you, and if you don't, maybe you will someday.
